Flow for focusing

6th May 2020 | my setup

I’ve been getting a lot of value out of Flow, a free pomodoro app. It’s a very clean and simple app which runs quietly in your menu bar and notifies you when to start breaks etc.

I was previously using Emphasis on the iPhone which I would also still recommend, but I’m remembering to start my sessions more reliably now it runs on the Mac.
