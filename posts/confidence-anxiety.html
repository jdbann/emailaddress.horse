CONFIDENCE/ANXIETY

14th June 2021

Over the weekend, I finished off an idea I had been playing with for the Tiny Library Jam. In the last few weeks, my therapist explained what anxiety was. It had been floating around for so long that I didn’t realise it was a nameable thing.

The exciting realisation was that anxiety was all about my perception of my actions, not whether or not they were valuable contributions.

So I present CONFIDENCE/ANXIETY, a simple hack for any RPG that uses a dice-roll for a skill-check or save:
