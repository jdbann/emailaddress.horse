Dungeons & Dragons

8th May 2020 | d&d

I love Dungeons & Dragons.

I don’t know what else needs saying. I’ve just spent a couple of hours interviewing my players. They’ve got such a strong relationship to the game already and had so much insight to share.

Sharing a passion like this with people has always been the way I most comfortably connect with new people. Writing here is much the same. I’m not writing on this blog to share my universal truths. I’m writing to see what other people think.

I used to keep things like my improv comedy, my Dungeons & Dragons, my bookbinding, and my horse facts to myself. And no-one showed an interest. Because there was nothing shown to them. So I’m writing this blog.

If you have thoughts about Dungeons & Dragons then please tell me. I love it so much and want to share the things I love with other people.
