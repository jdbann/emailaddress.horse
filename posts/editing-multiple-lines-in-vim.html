Editing multiple lines in vim

17th May 2020 | tips

Formatting a list recently, I finally looked up how to insert text in multiple lines in vim.

Jump in to visual block mode with Ctrl+v, select the column of text you want to insert into, press I to go to insert mode, type whatever you need to insert and then esc to return to command mode.

Again, I’m sharing this here to remind myself. I’m sure you’ve got some even cooler ways to do this kind of thing. Let me know on Twitter.
