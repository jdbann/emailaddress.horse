Public accountability

9th June 2021

I’m trying to building some of my routine and habits back up. The pandemic has shaken all of them up. But as I research more about a possible ADHD diagnosis, it seems important to do this personal gardening work to stop everything getting mentally overgrown.

It’s a tortured metaphor. But I’m trying to get some structure back. I’m starting with meditation. Specifically mindfulness as peddled by Headspace. Without a decent routine to hook this into, I’m turning to other powerful tools. And the biggest hammer in that box is accountability.

So I’ve setup a page on this site where I will be sharing my habit tracking. It will help for two reasons:

  • It was fun to make the page and I look forward to seeing how it grows and how I can improve it;
  • It is embarrassing to encourage people to take a look and then see that I have failed.

So please help me stick with my habits! Encouragement and/or nudges will be greatly appreciated.
